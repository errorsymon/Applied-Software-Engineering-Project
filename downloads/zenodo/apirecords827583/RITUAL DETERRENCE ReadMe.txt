The research will be based on a set of in-depth qualitative case studies investigating historical and contemporary contexts: nuclear deterrence in NATO-Warsaw Pact relations during the Cold War, NATO-Russia deterrence dynamics in the post-Cold War era, USA-Russia cyber and hybrid deterrence relations, USA-China deterrence relationship in the post-Cold War era, and memory-political deterrence in Eastern Europe. The research maps the ways deterrence is practiced and made sense of by relevant policy-making, military and diplomatic elites across the distinct areas and policy contexts of deterrence.People involved with sample collection, processing, analysis and/or submission: Prof. Maria MŠlksoo, Principal Investigator, University of Copenhagen, Department of Political Sciencemaria.malksoo@ifs.ku.dk Dr Cameron Hunter,Postdoctoral Research Fellow, University of Copenhagen, Department of Political Sciencecpa@ifs.ku.dk Thomas Fraise,Affiliated Researcher (postdoctoral level)thomas.fraise@sciencespo.frMariia VladymyrovaDoctoral Researcher, University of Copenhagen, Department of Political Sciencevla@ifs.ku.dk DATA-SPECIFIC INFORMATION FOR: RITUAL DETERRENCE_Maria MŠlksoo_Interview guide for Case Study 2_23Aug2023
